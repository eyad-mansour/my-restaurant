Welcome to Scott's Richmond

The opening of Scott’s Richmond in September 2022 marks the beginning of a new chapter. Scott’s of Mount Street, Mayfair, has defined London dining through countless changing decades and has a sparkling, storied past that is the stuff of local legend. Now it is expanding into a new location for the first time, the scene is set for a decadent new narrative. Sited on a westward meander of the Thames, some 15 winding river miles upstream of central London, Richmond has a history and magic all its own. Weeping willows caress the water with supple branches and herds of deer range freely amid the splendour of the royal park, just as they did when Elizabeth, I surveyed them from the grounds of Richmond Palace. Roaring into the town in a burst of champagne fizz, Scott’s will shake up its new riverside setting with a cocktail of old-world elegance and glamorous destination dining.
